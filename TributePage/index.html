Elon Musk

Investor, Engineer, and Inventor

Elon Musk a Canadian-American business investor who is very popular in the technological industry due to his many popular breakthrough's, most importantly electric cars. Very early on, Elon Musk set many goals to save humanity. A few of them included reducing carbon emissions by creating affordable electric cars and fast-tracking man reaching the moon.

Here is a timeline of Elon Musk's Life:

  • 1971: Born in South Africa
  • 1983: Creates his own video game and sells it for $500
  • 1988: Moves to Canada
  • 1989: Starts at a college in Ontario
  • 1994: Receives Wharton economics degree
  • 1995: Starts up the Zip2 company with his brother while programming the website himself
  • 1999: Sells Zip2 and receives $22 Million
  • 1999: Starts an online bank, X.com
  • 2000: Merges X.com with Confinity to form PayPal
  • 2001: Travels to Russia to buy a rocket ship so he can send it to Mars but negotiations eventually fail
  • 2002: Decides to build his own rockets since rockets were overpriced and starts a new company, SpaceX
  • 2002: Sells PayPal and earns $250 Million
  • 2004: Meets Tesla founders and invests in Tesla
  • 2006: First SpaceX rocket is launched and explodes
  • 2007: Second launch is also a failure
  • 2008: First Tesla Roadster is released to customers, 3rd launch of rocket fails, 4th launch of rocket is successful, SpaceX and Tesla are running out of cash, but Musk is able to escape bankruptcy for both companies
  • 2009: The first Model S is unveiled
  • 2011: Promises to send humans to Mars
  • 2012: Unveils the Tesla Model X and the first Model S is shipped
  • 2013: Publishes the design of the Hyperloop
  • 2015: Begins building Gigafactory, unveils Powerwall, and the first customers receive their Model X
  • 2016: Launches the largest rocket hes created so far, Falcon Heavy
